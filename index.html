// ABOUT

Built in silence for twelve months.

FORM3D.AI started in early 2025 as an internal research project. Engineers with backgrounds in computer vision, generative AI, and real-time graphics set out to answer a question: can a neural network learn to think in three dimensions from flat images alone?

After twelve months, the answer is yes. Our architecture doesn't reconstruct depth maps or stitch point clouds — it infers volume, topology, and articulation the way a sculptor reads form from a photograph.

The output is a fully rigged, textured, animation-ready 3D asset. No cleanup. No post-processing. Every model on this page was generated by the system — not modeled by hand.

Until now, this technology existed only inside our lab. To bring FORM3D.AI from research to product, we're seeking investment to fund infrastructure scaling, API development, and creator tools.

Early investors gain priority access to the platform. This isn't a pitch deck — it's a working technology looking for the right capital to scale.
