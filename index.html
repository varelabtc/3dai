Our Vision

The gap between imagination
and dimension

For decades, 3D content creation has been locked behind specialized tools, steep learning curves, and production timelines measured in weeks. Concept artists sketch ideas in hours that take modelers months to realize. That asymmetry is the problem we set out to solve.

Neuravox began in 2023 as a research initiative exploring whether neural networks could learn spatial reasoning from 2D inputs alone — not through depth estimation or point cloud reconstruction, but through a fundamentally different approach: training models to understand objects the way sculptors do, by inferring volume, structure, and surface from a single perspective.

After 18 months of closed research, we developed a proprietary architecture that doesn't just generate meshes — it understands topology, anticipates articulation points, and produces assets that are rigged, textured, and animation-ready from the first output.

The result is a system that bridges the gap between concept and production, giving creators, studios, and developers a tool that turns reference into reality without sacrificing quality or creative control.

Roadmap

Where we're headed

A transparent timeline of our research milestones, product development, and public release targets.

Phase 1
January — September 2024
Research & Closed Prototype
Foundational research on spatial inference from 2D inputs. Development of the core neural architecture. Internal benchmark testing against existing reconstruction methods. First successful end-to-end generation from single image to rigged mesh.
Core architecture Training pipeline Internal benchmarks Mesh topology solver
Phase 2
October 2024 — March 2025
Public Demonstration
Launch of the demonstrative platform showcasing generated outputs. Publication of technical overview and benchmark results. Community building and feedback collection from creators and studios. Partnership conversations with early adopters.

Phase 3
April — August 2025

Limited access program for selected creators, game studios, and VFX professionals. API integration for existing production pipelines. Iterative improvement based on real-world production feedback. Export support for major engines and DCC tools.

Impact

Reshaping how the world builds in 3D

The implications extend far beyond faster production. When the barrier between concept and creation disappears, entirely new categories of work become possible.

Game Development

Indie teams and AAA studios alike spend months on asset creation. Neuravox compresses that timeline from weeks to minutes, letting small teams compete with large-scale productions without compromising visual quality.

Film & VFX

Pre-visualization and rapid prototyping become immediate. Directors and VFX supervisors can iterate on spatial concepts in real time during pre-production, fundamentally changing how stories are planned and visualized.

Digital Products & E-Commerce

Product visualization, virtual try-on, and spatial commerce are constrained by the cost of 3D content. Automated generation unlocks 3D at scale — making immersive shopping experiences accessible to any brand, not just the largest.
